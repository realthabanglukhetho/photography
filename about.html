About me, in short

Welcome to the master photographer ThabangLukhetho. I'm a just a curious person who really likes to try and learn literally anything I come across and photography is one aspect of me.

I am interested in telling and arousing emotions through images. I love photographs that explicity communicate their nature but I prefer not to fixate on a specific style, i like to experiment and adopt different views depending on what is happening at that moment. I'm originally from a diverse South African township called SOSHANGUVE (SOTHO-SHANGANI-NGUNI-VENDA) and yep that is where it gets it name LOL, a full representation of 11 official SA languages.

The rest of me is told by things I do. If you see me around please come say hi.
